ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT CELL (EDC)

EDC is launched in our college with a view to encouraging students self-employment as a career option, providing training in entrepreneurship through modular courses, and increasing the relevance of management, particularly in the non-corporate and under managed sectors



Following are the objective of EDC:

  1. To create an environment for self-employment and entrepreneurship development through formal and non-formal programmes.
  2. To introduce the concept of entrepreneurship in curricula at diploma and degree levels.
  3. To develop management personnel at appropriate levels for the non-corporate and unorganized sectors like education, rural development, small-scale industry etc.
  4. To utilize the infrastructure facilities and technically trained manpower for the development of non-corporate and unorganized sectors.
  5. To promote employment opportunities.

Entrepreneurship Development Cell has been functioning in our institution since August 2017. EDC organises many programmes, like expert talks by successful entrepreneurs and industrial visits.The present coordinator of EDC is Smt SHEEBA M H Lecturer in Electronics Engineering.
